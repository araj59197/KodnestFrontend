About Kohinoor Jewels

Established in 1985, Kohinoor Jewels has been crafting exquisite jewelry pieces for over three decades. We specialize in traditional Indian designs with a modern touch, using only the finest materials including 22K gold, precious stones, and diamonds.

Our master craftsmen bring generations of expertise to every piece, ensuring that each item is not just jewelry, but a work of art that tells a story.
