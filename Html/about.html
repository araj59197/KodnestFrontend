Our Story

A Journey Through Time

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman Rajesh Kumar, Kohinoor Jewels began as a small workshop in the heart of Mumbai's jewelry district. What started with a vision to preserve traditional Indian craftsmanship has evolved into one of the most trusted names in fine jewelry.

Our founder's dedication to excellence and passion for intricate designs laid the foundation for what would become a legacy of timeless beauty. Today, we continue this tradition with the same commitment to quality and artistry that has defined us for nearly four decades.
